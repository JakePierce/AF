About Blue Nectar Yoga

Our schedule is filled with a variety of challenging inspirational classes with some of the best instructors in town. From our beginner yoga classes, like Rookie Vinyasa Flow or Hatha Yoga for Beginners, to our specialty classes like Sweet Prenatal Flow, Rock n’ Flow, or Restorative Yoga, our staff will make you feel right at home. The Bluetique features some of our favorite yoga lifestyle items from Jade, Manduka, Zum, and more – the essentials for every yogi. Parking is free in the garage behind the studio.

The sweetest thing? Blue Nectar Yoga is locally owned and owner-operated. We are committed to notch customer service and supporting the local Falls Church community. Whether you’re new to yoga or are a seasoned practitioner, we warmly welcome yogi’s of all varieties. Did we mention that we love what we do here?

We are yoga without attitude! Come on into Blue and feel the difference!

Class Descriptions

Blue Nectar Yoga offers a variety of challenging and inspirational classes designed to meet you where you are. Our core classes are rooted in vinyasa flow and traditional hatha yoga and provide the foundation for staying strong, balanced, and zen. Each class is categorized as either a level”1.0″, “2.0”, or “mixed level” to help you choose the pace or level of challenge that’s right for you. Specialty classes are all “mixed level” unless otherwise noted and are designed to compliment your yoga practice.

  1. Core Classes: Level 1.0

    2. Learn or refine foundational yoga postures to help build strength, stamina, and flexibility. Slower pace (when compared to “mixed level” and “level 2.0” classes) allows for more time to explore postures and potential modifications using props, i.e. blocks, straps, etc. These options are ideal for those who simply prefer a slower pace, are recovering from training or injury, or are new

    • Gentle Nectar

      • Meet the softer side of BNY. Gain strength, flexibility, and inner peace in a class foundational yoga postures with meditation and breathing. Mindful movements allow you to increase body awareness and improve range of motion.  Slower pace allows for more time to refine alignment as needed.

    • Rookie Vinyasa Flow

      • Not everyone wants to be called a beginner, but we all have to start somewhere. Whether you’re just starting a yoga practice or prefer sticking to the basics, this class will give you the physical and mental foundation for a safe, strong, injury-free vinyasa flow yoga practice. Learn or refine foundational yoga postures while the perfect tunes keep you moving and breathing. While no yoga experience is necessary, this class is ideal for those who are currently active.

    • Core Classes: Mixed Level

      • Newer students can refine foundational yoga postures while veteran yogis can play with variations. Pace is moderate with plenty of options for increasing or decreasing the level of challenge as needed. These options are ideal for those who are currently active or have some yoga experience.

    • Hatha Yoga

      • Increase body awareness, strength and flexibility while refining foundational yoga postures. This class will introduce more demanding postures that are sure to increase stamina. Slower pace (when compared to vinyasa flow classes) allows for more time spent holding postures and refining alignment. You’ll also find a greater focus on breath work and meditation.

    • Vinyasa Flow 60

      • Full of stress and short on time? This is Blue Nectar Yoga’s mixed level vinyasa flow class packaged into one tidy hour. We understand that tight schedules don’t always allow time for a longer yoga practice. Get in here and still get it done, because sometimes less is more.

    • Vinyasa Flow 75

      • Blue Nectar Yoga’s mixed levels vinyasa flow class. This is an energizing vinyasa flow class taught with healthy focus on pose alignment. Lots of options and modifications are provided, so you can explore mind/body while the perfect tunes keep you going. The 75 minutes (a more traditional class length) allows for a more complete inversion sequence and longer savasana.

    • Rock n' Flow

      • Get ready to shake your asana in this Blue Nectar Yoga mixed levels vinyasa flow class style. This is a sweaty flow set to rock n’ tunes. Lots of playful options are provided, so come ready to let loose!

  2. Core Classes: Level 2.0

    3. Take your practice to the next level as you explore variations of traditional yoga postures. You’ll find more vigorous practice overall with modifications to help you stretch your limits and strengthen your asana. A regular yoga practice is strongly encouraged.

    • Advanced Hatha Yoga (level 2.0)

      • Increase strength and stamina while exploring a wider variety of postures. Learn how to safely practice deeper backbends and/or more advanced arm balances, which are regularly taught in this class. A regular practice is highly recommended. Slower pace (when compared to vinyasa flow classes) allows for more time spent holding postures and refining alignment.

    • Vinyasa Flow 75 (level 2.0)

      • This is Blue Nectar Yoga’s level 2.o vinyasa flow yoga class. This is a vigorous class taught with a healthy focus on pose alignment. Explore variations of traditional postures and play with more intermediate to advanced arm balances while the perfect tunes keep you going.

    • Intro to Rocket Yoga (level 2.0)

      • A dynamic and fast paced style of yoga rooted in the Ashtanga yoga practice. Postures are combined and restructured in a fun and unique way. This class is designed for students with some yoga experience who are looking to get comfortable in the “Rocket” style.

    • Specialty Classes

      • Designed to compliment your yoga practice. All specialty classes are “mixed level” unless otherwise noted and will provide plenty of options for new and veteran yogis alike.

    • Restorative Yoga (level 1.0)

      • Your weekly dose of rest and recovery, BNY style. Most of us know how to push really hard – it’s the slowing down that’s often difficult. Here, you’ll find a grounding blend of restorative poses, yin, and self-myofascial release (SMR) techniques that will leave you feeling perfectly zen. No better way to relax and unwind.

    • Friday @ Blue!

      • TGIF! Consider this your “yoga du jour” each and every Friday. Each week will feature a specialty class taught by one of your favorite Blue Crew team members. Class styles and descriptions will be posted in advance. Get ready to relax, unwind, and let the week go!

    • Yoga Conditioning

      • Yoga and fitness unite! Build total body strength to help balance your asana. This fun, will include exercises using body weight, fitness bands, and balls. The perfect compliment to your weekly yoga practice.

    • Sweet Baby & Me

      • Babies are natural yogis! Bring in your Blue-tiful baby for an hour of connection, yoga, and fun. The community nature of this class will support you during the most challenging of journeys – parenthood! It is the perfect opportunity to get out, meet other parents, strengthen your own body with yoga poses, and play with your sweet baby at the same time. Babies 2 weeks through crawlers (inclusive) are welcome! This class is open to moms, dads, and caregivers alike.

    • Sweet Prenatal Flow

      • This BNY prenatal vinyasa flow has the active woman in mind. Mamas at any stage of pregnancy are encouraged to attend to feel the amazing benefits associated with a yoga practice and pregnancy. Focus on breath work, stretch out the sticky spots, and enjoy a few well-deserved relaxing postures. Prepare for labor and delivery and build a network of support. Taught by well-qualified prenatal instructors.

    • Yoga Play Date

      • Calling all parents who might enjoy combining their kids and yoga – in one festive room! Get out of the house and bring your little yogis in for a yoga play date. A vinyasa flow 60 class is held while children play and interact simultaneously with each other in the same space. Children of any age may attend and parents can tend to lil’ ones as needed. Don’t let anything stop you from practicing. Come on in and just get “in the moment”!

    • Yin Yoga

      • Complementary to active, or Yang, yoga classes (like vinyasa flow or traditional hatha), Yin yoga focuses on longer held postures to target the deep, dense connective tissues of the body. The aim is to improve circulation and flexibility around the joints. Props are used generously throughout the practice to help you find ultimate relaxation. You’ll also find a greater focus on breath work and meditation. Perfect for all
